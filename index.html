Run My Gunz

  1. Press Start Game to Play
  2. The police officer will require a random bribe amount at the start of the game.
  3. Make profit by selling your guns.
  4. You must match your profit with required bribe amount per round to win.
  5. If you make more profit than the bribe amount, jail will be waiting for you
  6. Happy Gun Running!
